Real Estate Investment Calculator

Explanation of Scenarios

1. Buy, Live, and Sell: This scenario calculates the financial outcome of purchasing a home, living in it for a specified number of years, and then selling it. It considers the initial purchase price, down payment, closing costs, mortgage payments, property taxes, home insurance, maintenance costs, HOA fees, inflation, appreciation rate, and selling costs (agent commission, transfer tax, fixed costs).

2. Buy, Live, Rent, and Sell: This scenario calculates the financial outcome of purchasing a home, living in it for a certain number of years, renting it out for additional years, and then selling it. The simulation includes rental income and costs such as vacancy rates and property management fees in addition to the factors considered in the first scenario. If the rental cash flow is positive, it will be invested in the market.

3. Invest: Instead of buying a home, you choose to invest the money that would have been used for the down payment and other homeownership costs of that property. The simulation calculates the growth of your investments over time and compares it with the cost of renting. If, the rental income pays for all the costs, only the remaining investment will grow as technically there is no more home cost!
